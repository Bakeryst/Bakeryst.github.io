FAQ

Question: What kind of music do you enjoy the most?
Answer: I enjoy game soundtracks ~ they’re immersive, emotional, and perfect for tasks or late-night overthinking.

Question: Any favorite games when it comes to music?
Answer: Absolutely. Undertale, Deltarune, and Nico Nextbots offer soundtracks that range from relaxing to chaotic brilliance.

Question: Isn’t it unusual to prefer soundtracks over popular music?
Answer: Not really. Soundtracks tell stories without lyrics — sometimes, they express more than words ever could (and no awkward lyrics to explain).

Question: Do you have an all-time favorite track?
Answer: I rotate between favorites, but any song that makes daily life feel like a cutscene deserves a replay.

Question: When do you usually listen to music?
Answer: While editing videos, working, helping at the store, or just reflecting. Basically, music is my daily background score.
